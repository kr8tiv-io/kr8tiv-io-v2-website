This form is basically our brain-dump catcher: the more you give us, the better the first design or proposal will be. Answer the optional questions if you want us to be smarter on the first pass; skip them if you’re in a rush. We love tiny jobs (yes — $10–$50 microjobs are absolutely a thing), weird experiments, and polished corporate work — no job too big, no job too small. Honest, clear answers = smarter work and faster results.

We’re humans who use modern tools — we’ll wire up the right systems, but we won’t lose the ability to pick up the phone at 10pm if that’s what it takes. Once you hit send we’ll review and get back with next steps — sometimes a quick email, sometimes a quote, sometimes a calendar link for an intro call.
